Pizza Recipe

This is the recipe on how to cook a pizza.

Ingredients

  • Flatbread
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spinach
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive Oil
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Add the pasta sauce flat to the flatbread
  3. Sprinkle the cheese onto the flatbread
  4. Add the olive oil
  5. Add the spinach and pepperoni to the flatbread
  6. Drizzle olive oil on top
  7. Put into oven for 15 minutes
  8. After 15 minutes, let it rest for another 15 minutes/li>
