Sandwich Recipe

This is the recipe on how to build the best sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Cold Cuts
  • Mayo
  • Pickles
  • Sandwich Sauce
  • Cheese
  1. Toast the bread
  2. Heat up the cold cut meats
  3. Put the lettuce flat first
  4. Then put the cold cuts on top of the lettuce
  5. Put the pickles on top
  6. Put mayo on top bread along with the cheese
  7. Finished
