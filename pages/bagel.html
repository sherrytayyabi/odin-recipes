Egg and cheese Bagel Recipe

This is the recipe on how to make the best bagel egg and cheese

Ingredients

  • Everything bagel
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Bacon
  • Salt/Pepper
  1. Toast the bagel
  2. Cook the egg
  3. Place egg onto bagel
  4. Put cheese on top of the egg
  5. Add bacon bits to the bagel
  6. Add salt and pepper on top
